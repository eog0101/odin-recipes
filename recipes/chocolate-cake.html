A Beautiful Chocalate Cake

This homemade chocolate cake recipe is super easy to prepare in one bowl for a deliciously moist and fluffy chocolate cake. This is the best chocolate cake I have ever had! Frost with your favorite frosting.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Directions

  1. Step 1: Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
  2. Step 2: Stir sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a large bowl.
  3. Step 3: Add eggs, milk, oil and vanilla; mix for 2 minutes on medium speed with an electric mixer.
  4. Step 4: Stir in the boiling water. The batter will be thin.
  5. Step 5: Pour evenly into the prepared pans.
  6. Step 6: Bake for 30 to 35 minutes in the preheated oven, until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Step 7: Cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove to a wire rack to cool completely.
  8. Step 8: Frost with your favorite frosting and enjoy!
